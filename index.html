Text2Sheets

Text2Sheets converts messy text, invoices, emails, and screenshots into clean spreadsheets within seconds. It detects structure, normalizes data, and delivers ready-to-use Excel or Google Sheets exports so teams stop retyping and start analyzing.

  • Instant recognition — tables, lists, key-value pairs detected automatically
  • Smart cleaning — removes duplicates, fixes formatting, aligns columns
  • Batch processing — upload hundreds of documents, get one unified sheet
  • Privacy-first — your data never trains our models

ÖzelEğitim.app is the management hub for Turkish private tutoring centers. Student progress, parent communication, scheduling, and payments live in one mobile ready platform built for local expectations, so teams focus on teaching instead of juggling tools.

  • Parent portal — instant updates, grades, attendance in one app

  • Automated billing — invoices, payments, reminders handled
